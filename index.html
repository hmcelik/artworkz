About Us

ArtworkZ Infinity is a BSC project aiming to be an Artist Hub / NFT Platform for everyone. ArtworkZ Infinity main concept was built thru the spread of NFT Arts in every space available. The “boom” of Crypto and NFT Arts was the main catalyst for the founder and developers to form this team and the development of the concept. The opportunity for this project is limitless and the team is here to push this 100% to the moon.

Mission

ArtworkZ Infinity aims to house NFT Artists and give them a place to shine. ArtworkZ Infinity’s main mission is to give opportunities to under-rated artists for the real value of their Artworks.

ArtworkZ Infinity also aims to give investors their a chance to open up their thoughts and opinions on the NFTs they will be investing in. We will be the first to have customizable NFTs in all platforms.
